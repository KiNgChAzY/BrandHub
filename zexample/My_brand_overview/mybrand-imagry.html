camera_alt Subject Matter Guidance

  • Focus on people over products (when applicable) to emphasize our human-centric approach.
  • Show real environments like active warehouses, modern offices, or transport hubs.
  • Avoid clichés like handshakes against white backgrounds or generic "thumbs up" poses.

crop Composition & Treatment

  • Favor wide shots with breathing room to convey scale and openness.
  • Use depth of field naturally to draw focus to the subject without blurring context entirely.
  • Avoid aggressive cropping that removes essential context.

* Tip: Always leave sufficient negative space for typography when images are used in marketing headers.
