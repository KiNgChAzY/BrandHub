Language Guidelines

bolt

Use Active Voice

Put the subject first. Say "We launched the feature" instead of "The feature was launched by us." It's stronger and shorter.

chat_bubble_outline

Avoid Slang

Our audience is global. Avoid idioms or local slang that might not translate well or could be confusing.

mood

Emojis? Sparingly.

Acceptable in Slack and casual marketing. Never in legal docs, error messages, or core product UI.
